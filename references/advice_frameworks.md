# Advice Frameworks

## Career Advice by Type

### NT Types (INTJ, INTP, ENTJ, ENTP)
- Leverage strategic thinking for leadership/innovation roles
- Watch for analysis paralysis (especially INTx)
- Build people skills as force multiplier
- Best roles: Strategy, architecture, R&D, consulting

### NF Types (INFJ, INFP, ENFJ, ENFP)
- Channel vision into mission-driven work
- Set boundaries to prevent burnout from over-empathizing
- Use storytelling ability for influence and leadership
- Best roles: Coaching, product, design, education, content

### ST Types (ISTJ, ISTP, ESTJ, ESTP)
- Lean into execution excellence and reliability
- Develop comfort with ambiguity and long-term vision
- Use practical skills as foundation for leadership
- Best roles: Operations, engineering, finance, management

### SF Types (ISFJ, ISFP, ESFJ, ESFP)
- Build on people-centered strengths
- Develop strategic thinking alongside interpersonal skills
- Use harmony-building as leadership superpower
- Best roles: HR, healthcare, customer success, community

## Communication Advice Framework

### Blind Spot Detection
For each type, common communication blind spots:

- **E types**: May dominate conversations, miss quiet signals
- **I types**: May under-communicate, seem disengaged
- **S types**: May miss big picture, get lost in details
- **N types**: May skip practical steps, seem unrealistic
- **T types**: May seem cold, miss emotional context
- **F types**: May avoid necessary conflict, over-personalize
- **J types**: May seem controlling, rush to closure
- **P types**: May seem unreliable, resist commitment

### Improvement Actions
Each blind spot maps to a specific 30-day practice:
1. Identify the pattern in recent conversations
2. Set a specific behavioral experiment
3. Track results in a simple journal
4. Review and adjust after 2 weeks

## Growth Exercises by Dimension

### For E/I Balance
- **E developing I**: 10-min daily silent reflection, journaling before sharing
- **I developing E**: One unsolicited conversation per day, think-aloud practice

### For S/N Balance
- **S developing N**: "What if..." brainstorm 5 min/day, read about trends
- **N developing S**: Detail-first planning exercise, 5 senses grounding

### For T/F Balance
- **T developing F**: "How might they feel?" pause before feedback
- **F developing T**: Pro/con list before decisions, separate data from feelings

### For J/P Balance
- **J developing P**: Leave one thing unplanned per day, explore without agenda
- **P developing J**: Time-box one task, make one irreversible decision per week

## Relationship Insights

### Communication Style Matches
- Same type: Easy understanding, risk of echo chamber
- Opposite type: Growth potential, higher friction
- Adjacent type: Balanced growth with manageable friction

### Energy Management
- Identify energy drains vs energy gains in conversation patterns
- Map social battery indicators from chat frequency/length patterns
- Recommend energy management strategies based on E/I score

## Report Structure

The final advice report follows this structure:

1. **MBTI Result** — Type + confidence + dimensional scores
2. **Personality Snapshot** — 3-sentence summary of who you are
3. **Superpowers** — Top 3 strengths observed in conversations
4. **Growth Edges** — Top 3 areas for development
5. **Career Lens** — How your type maps to work
6. **Communication Guide** — Blind spots + improvement actions
7. **Relationship Insights** — Social style + compatibility notes
8. **30-Day Challenge** — One specific growth experiment
